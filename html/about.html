Welcome to Our Car Detailing Service

We’re two car enthusiasts — Alex and Tom — who share a strong passion for vehicles and everything about them. With training and hands-on experience in automotive repair, we’ve developed a deep appreciation for bringing cars back to life both mechanically and cosmetically.

We started this business not just to offer high-quality detailing, but to connect with other car lovers and deliver a premium, personalized experience. Whether it’s restoring a vehicle’s original shine or simply helping someone fall back in love with their ride, we’re here to make it happen — one detail at a time.
